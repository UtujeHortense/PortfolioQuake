About

The Balance board project was primarirly a mechatronics project as part of my Erasmus year. I worked on this project together with three other collaborators. I was in charge of the programming part and there fore will only discribe my part of the work.
The solution that we aimed for was a small robot that can maintain a ball on a flat plate even when disturbed by either movement or human interaction.
